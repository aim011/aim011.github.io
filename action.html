Take Action! Make a Change!

Big Actions:

  • Get an electric car
  • Get solar panels on your house
  • Grow your own food in a garden
  • Switch out your shower head for an eco friendly model to save water
  • Upgrade to rechargeable batteries
  • Switch from a non-native grass lawn to a native bushes, wildflowers, and trees
  • Donate to a Conservation Organization or Environmental Group

Facts

So many little decisions can be part of sustainable living!

Activity Sustainable Option Non-sustainable Option Why?
Getting a burger Veggie Burger Cheeseburger A cheeseburger takes about 3,000 liters of water to produce compared to a veggie burger's 180 liters
Driving Electric Car Gas Car A gas car emits 350 grams of CO2 per mile compared to electric car's about 200 grams per mile
Energy Renewable Energy Fossil Fuels Not only are renewable resources better for the atmosphere but they are also significantly cheaper!
Lightbulbs LEDs Regular bulbs LED bulbs use 80-90% less energy than regular bulbs
Packaging Glass Plastic Glass can be recycled repeatedly without quality loss
